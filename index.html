How it works

Simple. Automatic. Effective.

Three steps. Fully set up for you. Running within 48 hours.

1

Patient enquiry comes in

A patient messages your WhatsApp, fills in a form on your website, or enquires about a treatment. Their details are captured automatically.

2

AI replies instantly

Within seconds, your AI receptionist sends a warm, personalised message. It answers questions about treatments, handles concerns, and moves them towards booking.

3

Patient books a consultation

The AI sends your booking link at exactly the right moment. The patient picks a time. You see the appointment in your calendar. No chasing, no missed calls.

If they don't reply, we follow up automatically.
After 10 minutes of silence, the AI sends a gentle follow-up. Most patients convert at this stage.
