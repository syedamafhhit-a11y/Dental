Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how and why we use cookies on this website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use only essential and performance cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Understand how visitors navigate the site (anonymously)
  • Improve page performance and usability

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or any form of aggressive user tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

Our contact form uses Formspree to process submissions. Formspree may use cookies to function correctly. We use Google Fonts to load our typefaces. Neither service is used for advertising or data selling.

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable or clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of this website.
